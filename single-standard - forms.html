Groupie.

  • By Sheena Estrada
  • March 23, 2023
  • LifestyleEveryday Life

Stan Culture. It can easily change someone's life in just a blink of an eye. Well, it is dedicating majority of your life for someone you look up to. It is fun, but it can also bring some bad things to you. I mean, aren't all things like that?

For the fun part, it was really nice listening to their music and contents. Music has become another language of us, and it has become another way to communicate and connect to others. With this being said, I have made some friends because we share the same interest. This specific fandom that I am in are definitely a weird bunch, but I relate to them, and we understand each other. Age also doesn't matter. I have met someone who is much younger than me, and also someone much older than me. I think it is nice that people get together because of these little things.

For the bad things, they're not THAT bad. Like yes, fanwars are always there (even inside the fandom),and a lot of issues regarding the idols, online bullying, crazy stalkers, and even as far as doxxing, but they're not that bad. Okay, maybe they are, BUT I think they are inevitable though. There are bad things eveywhere. Although I must admit that being a fan has some detrimental effects on a person, especially someone who is very active as one on the internet.

All of this, I think, are a part of a human journey. You get to experience joy and hardship in everything, because that is just how life is wired.
